FAQ
Common questions.
Is there a free trial? +
Yes — every plan comes with a 4-day free trial. No credit card needed to start. You'll only be charged after the trial ends if you choose to continue.
What's included in the lifetime plan? +
Everything. All features, all future updates, forever. Pay once and it's yours — no subscription, no recurring charges, no nasty surprises.
How accurate is the food database? +
We use a large, community-maintained food database with over 1 million entries. The barcode scanner pulls verified nutritional data directly from product labels for the most accurate results.
Can I cancel my subscription? +
Yes, at any time through your Apple ID settings in the App Store. Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period.
Does CalMaster work without internet? +
Core logging features work offline. Your data syncs when you're back online. Some features like the barcode scanner require a connection to look up new products.
Is my data private? +
Yes. We collect only what's needed to run the app. We never sell your data to third parties. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.
