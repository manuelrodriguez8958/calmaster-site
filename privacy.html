Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

CalMaster ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the CalMaster mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address when you register
  • Health & Fitness Data: Height, weight, age, gender, calorie goals, food logs, exercise logs
  • Usage Data: App interactions, feature usage, streak data
  • Device Identifiers: User ID assigned upon registration

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the CalMaster service
  • To calculate calorie goals and nutritional insights
  • To send milestone emails at 7, 30, 90, 180, and 365-day streaks
  • To enable social features such as friend challenges and leaderboards

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, hosted on AWS infrastructure. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — Database and authentication
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management
  • Resend — Transactional email delivery
  • Anthropic — AI-powered food and exercise analysis

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your data with advertisers. Data is only shared with third-party services listed above as necessary to operate the app.

6. Your Rights

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. Children's Privacy

CalMaster is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email.
